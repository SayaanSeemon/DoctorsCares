DoctorsCare’s world-class, Centres of Excellence (COE) are an amalgamation of experienced doctors, state of the art technology, and the highest level of patient care and treatment. Our COE’s ensure that every aspect of your care is seamless and the team of experts work together to provide the care you need.

Ambulance Services

Our hospital provides 24/7 emergency ambulance services equipped with advanced life support systems and staffed by highly trained paramedics. Whether it’s a medical emergency, trauma care, or patient transfer, our fleet ensures safe, rapid, and reliable transportation to and from the hospital.

Emergency Rooms

Our Emergency Room is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, providing immediate medical care for patients experiencing serious or life-threatening conditions. Staffed by experienced emergency physicians, nurses, and support teams, we are equipped to handle a wide range of emergencies with speed, skill, and compassion.

Free Check-ups

we care about your well-being. That’s why we offer free health checkup camps to help you stay informed and proactive about your health. Our checkups are conducted by qualified medical professionals and include basic screenings to detect early signs of common health issues.
